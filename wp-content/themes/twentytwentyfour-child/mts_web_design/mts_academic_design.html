Preserving Wisdom Through Language

Following the tradition of great translators, we make classical Tibetan Buddhist texts accessible to practitioners worldwide, offering authentic dharma at cost-only prices since 2015.

View Projects Get In Touch

Our Sacred Mission

Guided by the vision of Drupon Khen Rinpoche, we continue the noble tradition of making profound Buddhist teachings accessible across linguistic boundaries.

"Having travelled widely in both the East and the West, I came to see the importance of good translators and translations. Many practitioners do not have a lot of money because they do not spend their time making money, they practise. Therefore, it can be difficult for them to acquire texts."

"Someone who conveys the Dharma in their own language to their fellow countrymen, is doing something wonderful and very important. With this motivation, I have been encouraging some people to become translators."

— Drupon Khen Rinpoche Karma Lhabu, Founder
💰

Cost-Only Principle

We charge only the equivalent of publishing costs, adding no profit margin. This ensures authentic dharma remains accessible to sincere practitioners who dedicate their lives to study and practice rather than wealth accumulation.

🧘

Practitioner-Scholars

Our twenty-plus translators study and practice under Rinpoche's guidance at Thrangu Sekhar Retreat Center. As Rinpoche reminds us, "dharma is more than words" - our translations must carry the feeling and blessing of dharma.

📚

Scholarly Excellence

We work closely with Khenpo Wangchuk to clarify difficult points, receiving detailed instructions on works under translation. We focus on treatises by masters universally accepted as learned and genuine.
