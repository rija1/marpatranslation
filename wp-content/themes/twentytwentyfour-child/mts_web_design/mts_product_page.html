About This Translation

This comprehensive translation of Jamgon Kongtrul's "nges don sgron me" represents years of careful scholarly work by our translator-practitioners at Thrangu Sekhar Retreat Center. Working under the direct guidance of Drupon Khen Rinpoche, this translation captures both the intellectual precision and spiritual depth of the original Tibetan text.

The Great Seal (Mahamudra) preliminaries are essential practices that prepare the mind for direct realization of its true nature. Kongtrul's masterful exposition provides practitioners with clear instructions on meditation techniques, philosophical understanding, and practical guidance for integrating these teachings into daily life.

This text serves as an indispensable companion for serious Mahamudra practitioners, offering profound insights into the nature of consciousness and the path to liberation. The translation includes extensive footnotes, glossary of terms, and contextual commentary to support both beginning and advanced students.
