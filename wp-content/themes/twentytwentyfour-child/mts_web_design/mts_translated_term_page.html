A Melodious Longing Plea To The Kagyu Teachers

Translated by: Rija Ratinahirana

Liturgical Text
English Translation
Original Tibetan Context
།ངེས་དོན་བསྟན་པའི་བདག་པོ། །ཆོས་རྒྱལ་ཟླ་འོད་གཞོན་ནུ།
།སྲས་བཅས་རྒྱལ་བའི་ཕྲིན་ལས། །དཔལ་ལྡན་ཀརྨ་བཀའ་བརྒྱུད།
English Translation
Master at expounding the true meaning,
Dharma King, Youthful Moonlight.
Activity of the Victorious and heirs,
Glorious Karma Kagyu.
Translator's Commentary
Chose this over 'definitive meaning' to keep the line short. The term ངེས་དོན་ (nges don) refers to the ultimate, non-conceptual truth as opposed to provisional teachings. In this liturgical context, it emphasizes the master's ability to teach the deepest level of Buddhist understanding.
