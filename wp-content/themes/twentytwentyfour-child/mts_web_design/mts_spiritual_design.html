Our Sacred Mission

Founded with compassion in 2015, we honor Drupon Khen Rinpoche's vision of making profound dharma teachings accessible to all sincere practitioners.

"Having travelled widely in both the East and the West, I came to see the importance of good translators and translations. Many practitioners do not have a lot of money because they do not spend their time making money, they practise."

"Someone who conveys the Dharma in their own language to their fellow countrymen, is doing something wonderful and very important. With this motivation, I have been encouraging some people to become translators."

‚Äî Drupon Khen Rinpoche Karma Lhabu, Compassionate Founder

Practice Before Profit

We offer texts at cost-only prices, understanding that sincere practitioners dedicate their lives to dharma rather than wealth accumulation. Every teaching should be accessible to the earnest heart.

Translators as Practitioners

Our twenty-plus translators study and practice under Rinpoche's loving guidance at Thrangu Sekhar Retreat Center, Nepal. They embody the teachings they translate, ensuring authentic transmission.
